# User Story & Requirements

## User Story

**As a** president of an Online Traveling Agency, which sells flight and hotel bookings on the web site,
**I want to** have a SEO evaluation system, which automatically gets the SEO scores of designated pages and shows them on a dashboard, based on the past SEO improvement measurements and their results,
**So that** I can monitor the current SEO scores of the target pages and check the effects of the measurement for SEO improvement from the dashboard.

## Advanced Requirements

As a SEO specialist, I want to get a suggentstion from the system about what measurements can increase the SEO score so that I can effectively implement the SEO improvements based on the past strategies.
